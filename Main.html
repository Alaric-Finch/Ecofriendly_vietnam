Overview

"Ha Long Bay and Cat Ba Island are among Vietnam's most iconic destinations, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty and adventure. Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is renowned for its emerald waters and thousands of towering limestone karsts. Just a short boat ride away, Cat Ba Island invites you to explore its lush national park, hidden beaches, and vibrant local culture. Together, these two destinations provide an unforgettable experience of Vietnam's coastal charm."
